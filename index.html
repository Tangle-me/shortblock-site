STEPS 2 & 3 OF 3 — ANDROID

Install on Android — takes 2 minutes

1

Download ShieldKid to your child's phone

Send this link to your child's phone, or connect the phone to your computer and copy the file across.

⬇ Download ShieldKid.apk

File size: ~4MB · Safe to download · No Google Play needed

2

Open the file and tap Install

On the child's phone, open the downloaded file. Android will ask "Allow install from unknown sources" — tap Settings → Allow, then go back and tap Install.

3

Set your parent PIN — app hides itself

The app opens and asks you to set a PIN. Choose any 4+ digit number only you know. Tap Activate Protection. The app disappears from the home screen. Protection is live.
